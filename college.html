About us

Best Junior College in Mumbai

Durgadevi Saraf Junior College (DSJC) is one of the leading junior colleges in Mumbai for FYJC and SYJC students. It follows the Higher Secondary Certificate Examination of Maharashtra Board and is founded on the motto of ‘Knowledge is enhanced by humility alone’. DSJC offers Science, Commerce and Arts streams with a focus on value-based education. Being a part of the Rajasthani Sammelan Educational Trust, this best college after 10th completes nearly four decades of existence. It’s an institution of higher learning that puts an emphasis on human enrichment and development.
